        Natural Language Processing     -- Is human language computable?

            - language modelling: produce text that looks human like chatGPT

            - summarization: summarize an article of text in 2 words

            - sentiment analysis: is the writer of the article happy or angry?

            - ranking: produce the most relevant results matching a query like search engines or recommendation engines

                - unsupervised recommendation: produce results matching a query without using personal data
